### 3. Confirmed the Use of Standardized Flowcharts to Support Project Development

- clarify the operating logic of each functional module;
- show the interaction between the front end, back end, and question bank content;
- help team members quickly understand the overall system structure;
- clarify the person responsible for each part and their scope of responsibility;
- reduce repeated communication and improve development efficiency;
- provide supporting materials for the later project report, presentation, and demonstration.

The team believed that flowcharts can help everyone understand the overall development route of the project more clearly. They can also make it easier to identify whether a problem belongs to the front end, back end, question bank content, or system logic.
